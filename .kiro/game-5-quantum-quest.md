# Game Concept: Quantum Quest

## Overview
An interactive quantum mechanics learning game where students explore the bizarre world of quantum physics through hands-on experiments and puzzles.

## Core Mechanics
- **Wave-Particle Duality Puzzles**: Switch between wave and particle modes to solve challenges
- **Superposition Challenges**: Objects exist in multiple states until observed
- **Entanglement Missions**: Connect particles across space and time
- **Uncertainty Principle Gameplay**: Balance position and momentum measurements

## Learning Objectives
- Understand quantum superposition
- Grasp wave-particle duality
- Learn about quantum entanglement
- Explore the uncertainty principle
- Discover quantum tunneling
