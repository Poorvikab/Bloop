# Game Concept: Ecosystem Empire

## Overview
A biology and ecology simulation game where students build and balance complex ecosystems, learning about food chains, biodiversity, and environmental science.

## Core Gameplay
- Start with a barren landscape
- Introduce species one by one
- Balance predator-prey relationships
- Manage resources and climate
- Respond to environmental challenges

## Learning Objectives
- Understand food webs and energy flow
- Learn about biodiversity importance
- Grasp ecosystem balance concepts
- Explore climate change impacts
- Discover conservation strategies

## Game Mechanics

### 1. Species Management
- Introduce producers (plants)
- Add primary consumers (herbivores)
- Include secondary consumers (carnivores)
- Manage decomposers
- Balance population dynamics

### 2. Environmental Factors
- Control water availability
- Adjust temperature and climate
- Manage soil quality
- Introduce weather events
- Handle pollution levels

### 3. Challenges & Events
- Invasive species arrival
- Natural disasters
- Disease outbreaks
- Climate change scenarios
- Human impact events

### 4. Research & Discovery
- Unlock new species
- Discover symbiotic relationships
- Learn adaptation mechanisms
- Study extinction events
- Explore biome diversity

## Biomes to Explore
- Tropical Rainforest
- Desert
- Ocean
- Tundra
- Grassland
- Freshwater
- Coral Reef
- Temperate Forest

## Educational Integration
- Real species data
- Actual ecological relationships
- Current conservation issues
- Climate science facts
- Biodiversity statistics

## Progression System
- Unlock new biomes
- Expand ecosystem size
- Discover rare species
- Achieve balance milestones
- Complete conservation challenges

## Multiplayer Features
- Trade species with friends
- Collaborative ecosystem building
- Conservation competitions
- Share successful ecosystems
