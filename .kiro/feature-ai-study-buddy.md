# Feature Planning: AI Study Buddy

## Concept
A personalized AI companion that learns alongside the student, provides encouragement, and adapts its personality to match student preferences.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Conversational Learning
- Natural language Q&A
- Socratic method questioning
- Explain concepts in multiple ways
- Provide analogies and examples
- Admit when uncertain and research together

### 2. Emotional Intelligence
- Detect frustration and offer breaks
- Celebrate achievements
- Provide encouragement during struggles
- Adjust tone based on student mood
- Remember past conversations and progress

### 3. Study Session Management
- Suggest optimal study times
- Implement Pomodoro technique
- Track focus and attention
- Recommend breaks
- Create study schedules

### 4. Personalization
- Customizable avatar and voice
- Adjustable personality traits
- Preferred communication style
- Interest-based conversation topics
- Cultural and language preferences

## Technical Architecture
- Large language model integration
- Emotion detection algorithms
- Memory and context management
- Voice synthesis and recognition
- Avatar animation system

## Interaction Modes
- **Text Chat**: Quick questions and answers
- **Voice Conversation**: Natural dialogue
- **Video Avatar**: Animated character interaction
- **Screen Sharing**: Collaborative problem-solving
- **Whiteboard Mode**: Visual explanations

## Privacy & Safety
- Age-appropriate responses
- Content filtering
- Conversation monitoring
- Parental controls
- Data encryption

## Gamification
- Level up your study buddy
- Unlock new personalities
- Earn buddy customization items
- Buddy achievements and badges
- Study streak rewards

## Integration Points
- Works across all Bloop modules
- Appears in games as guide
- Assists with flashcard creation
- Helps plan study roadmaps
- Reviews quiz performance together
